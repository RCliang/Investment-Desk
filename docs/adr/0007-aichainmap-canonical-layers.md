# 0007 — aichainmap.com as canonical Layer→Sub-industry authority

**Status**: accepted

ADR-0002 fixed the five Layer set but left Sub-industry composition open.
Two paths were on the table: (a) reverse-engineer Sub-industries from East
Money / iwencai concept boards, or (b) adopt an external authority verbatim.
The first path was tried (see `backend/scripts/explore_layers.py`,
`explore_ai_themes.py`, and the resulting `layer_exploration.json` /
`layer_4_5_iwencai.json`) and produced a noisy, A-share-only, concept-board-
flavored view of the chain that diverges from the reference the product is
modeled on.

This ADR commits to path (b): aichainmap.com's atlas is the canonical
source-of-truth for **Layer → Sub-industry → Company** membership in v1.

## Decision

aichainmap.com's "五层蛋糕" atlas (site update **2026·06·17**, fetched
**2026-06-24**) is the **canonical seed** for Sub-industry membership:

| Layer | Sub-industries | Visible companies | Est. total (visible + hidden) |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| I  能源与电力 (Energy)              |  5 |  49 | ~131 |
| II 芯片系统 (Chip Systems)         | 12 | 108 | ~251 |
| III AI基础设施 (AI Infrastructure) | 10 |  93 | ~239 |
| IV AI基础模型 (Foundation Models)  |  4 |  41 | ~170 |
| V  AI应用 (AI Applications)        | 17 | 166 | ~399 |
| **Total**                          | **48** | **457** | **~1190** |

- **Seed artifact**: `backend/data/aichainmap_seed.json` (generated by
  `backend/scripts/parse_atlas.py` from `backend/data/atlas_raw.txt`).
- **339 unique companies** are visible; the remainder are cross-references
  (科大讯飞 appears in 9 Sub-industries; 阿里巴巴 in 7).
- **Stream markers** (上游/中游/下游) are preserved on Layers I–IV. Layer V
  is flat in the source dump — no stream assignment.
- **iwencai and East Money** become **backfill data sources**, not
  classification authorities. They fill in real-time price, 研报, 资金流,
  融资融券, etc. for the companies already in the seed.

### What is *not* canonical

- **Sub-industry display names.** The public atlas dump does not contain
  Sub-industry *names* — only company clusters. The 48 zh names in
  `aichainmap_seed.json` (e.g., "半导体设备", "AI大模型平台",
  "人形机器人核心零部件") are **inferred** from the visible company set
  and hardcoded in `parse_atlas.py::SUBINDUSTRY_NAMES`. They are a starting
  point for editorial review, not authoritative. Expect them to be amended
  during Canonical review.
- **The hidden company set.** Each group carries `hidden_count` (the site's
  own `+ N` ellipsis) but the hidden companies themselves are behind a login
  wall. The seed only contains the server-rendered visible companies; the
  hidden set will be filled in by editorial research or future scraping.
- **A-share-only enrichment.** aichainmap mixes CN (323), HK (62), and US
  (72) companies. HK and US listings enter the seed as **Reference
  Companies** (per CONTEXT.md), not investable Listings.

## Considered options

- **(A) aichainmap as canonical, iwencai/EM as backfill** — accepted.
  Defensible reference, matches the product's stated model, cheap to seed,
  cleanly separates editorial structure (aichainmap) from market data
  (iwencai/EM). Risk: source is a single external site that could change
  shape or paywall further.
- **(B) Reverse-engineer from EM concept boards + iwencai** — rejected.
  Tried in prior sessions. East Money `push2/clist` was IP-blocked (skill
  #18); iwencai `query2data` returned 531 + 1061 companies across 20
  themes, but the themes are concept-board flavored
  ("DeepSeek概念股", "人形机器人概念股") and do not map cleanly to
  Sub-industries. Result was noisy and diverged from aichainmap's
  structure.
- **(C) Hand-author all 48 Sub-industries from scratch** — rejected.
  High editorial burden, slower time-to-seed, no external defensibility.

## Consequences

- `aichainmap_seed.json` is the **single source-of-truth** for the initial
  Sub-industry tree. DB seed migrations load from this file.
- **Parser limitations are accepted for v1.** The atlas dump uses
  whitespace as both within-name and between-companies separator, so
  multi-word US names (e.g., "Bloom EnergyBEE") may be truncated to their
  last token ("Energy"). Tickers are always captured correctly. US
  companies are Reference Companies that will be enriched during backfill,
  so this is tolerable.
- **iwencai query2data remains valuable** for two backfill tasks:
  (1) enrichment of Layer 4–5 Sub-industries with concept-board coverage
  breadth; (2) theme-based 研报 search. It does **not** define Sub-industry
  membership.
- **Amendments require an ADR.** If editorial review finds that aichainmap
  has mis-classified a Sub-industry (e.g., put a company in the wrong
  Layer), the fix is an ADR that cites the 研报 evidence — not a silent
  edit to `aichainmap_seed.json`.
- **Re-sync policy.** The aichainmap site updates roughly monthly
  ("2026·06·17" at fetch time). Re-fetching `atlas_raw.txt` quarterly and
  diffing against the prior dump is the expected refresh cadence; material
  diffs trigger a new ADR.

## Open questions (deferred)

- **Sub-industry names.** The 48 inferred names need editorial pass before
  Canonical status. Defer to a v1.1 curation task.
- **Stream markers on Layer V.** aichainmap renders Layer V flat. Should
  we impose our own 上游/中游/下游 split? Defer — flat is acceptable for v1.
- **Hidden companies.** Do we attempt to recover them via scraping the
  authenticated `/subindustry` pages, or leave them for editorial research?
  Defer — visible set is enough to ship v1.
